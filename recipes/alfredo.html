Alfredo Sauce

Description

This Alfredo sauce recipe always turns out rich and creamy! A delicious way to transform plain fettuccine pasta or steamed vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 1/2 cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over low heat. Stir in cream and simmer for 5 minutes.
  2. Add cheese and garlic; whisk continuously until heated through. Stir in parsley and serve.
